Canteens at College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET)


CET boasts a vibrant array of canteens catering to the diverse tastes and preferences of its bustling student body. Nestled amidst the sprawling campus, these canteens serve as much more than mere dining spots; they are hubs of social interaction, where students converge to unwind, fuel up, and exchange ideas. With an emphasis on quality, affordability, and variety, CET's canteens strive to meet the culinary needs of students, faculty, and visitors alike.


1. What types of cuisines are available at CET's canteens?

At CET's canteens, you'll find a delightful fusion of cuisines ranging from traditional Indian fare to continental delicacies. Whether you're craving steaming hot dosas or craving a cheesy pizza, there's something to satisfy every palate.


2. Are there options for students with dietary restrictions?


Absolutely! CET's canteens are committed to inclusivity, offering a range of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. From hearty salads to protein-packed lentil dishes, students with dietary restrictions can enjoy delicious meals without compromise.
